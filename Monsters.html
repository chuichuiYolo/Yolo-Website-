Concept

A thorn in the side, piercing through the mask on the face, piercing through the repression in the heart, piercing through the madness of repression.


A thorn in the body, piercing the surface of the camouflage, piercing the dark clouds overhead, piercing the gap between each other.


In this noisy world where people can't tell the difference between the north and the south, there are so many hidden and uncomfortable feelings that are repressed in the bottom of the heart, screaming for madness and catharsis. The nerves that have been repressed for so long need to be stimulated by pain, and the sting gives people the thrill of madness. This is the Eden of madness, the garden of the mad. In this brief orgy, everyone is a wanderer, a hollow man, free of responsibility, free of constraints, free from the responsibilities imposed by society, free to unleash the madness repressed in his heart.
